 🚍 TransitOps-ERP

# Overview

TransitOps ERP is an Enterprise Resource Planning platform designed to digitize and simplify transportation and operational management processes.

The platform provides a centralized solution for managing daily operations, resources, employees, safety activities, and business workflows efficiently. 
It helps organizations reduce manual work, improve data management, and make better decisions through organized and accessible information.



# Problem Statement

Many transportation and operational organizations still depend on manual processes for managing vehicles, employees, safety records, and operational data. 
This leads to inefficient workflows, data duplication, and difficulty in monitoring real-time activities.

TransitOps ERP addresses these challenges by providing an integrated platform that connects different operational departments in one system.



# Key Features

- User authentication and secure access management
- Centralized operational dashboard
- Transportation and resource management
- Employee information management
- Safety management system
- Data tracking and monitoring
- Reports and analytics for better decision-making



# Objectives

- To automate transportation and operational workflows
- To reduce dependency on manual record management
- To provide centralized data access
- To improve efficiency and productivity
- To enable better monitoring and decision-making
